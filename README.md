# Factions
![alt text](https://apex-misc.s3.amazonaws.com/Images/Factions+Thumbnail.png)

Apex Hosting Factions GameType

Introducing our factions gametype, In factions, players can band together to form factions and stake their claim on an area of land to build their base and deposit resources.
